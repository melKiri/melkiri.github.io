About

I'm a systems thinker first, designer second. My work lives at the structural layer — the foundations that make everything else possible. I'm most useful when the problem is complex, the scale is large, and there's no clear playbook yet.

At Pushpay I've spent three years building the infrastructure that lets 13 designers work across 7 products without stepping on each other. That means token architecture, AI tooling, accessibility systems, and bridging the gap between design decisions and engineering reality.

I believe senior design skill is no longer just building well. It's knowing how to make a system scale — with the right mix of automation, judgment, and taste.
